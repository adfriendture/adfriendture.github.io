Data Deletion Request

Effective January 1, 2026  ·  Framework Foundry LLC

Your data belongs to you. You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation to your email address.

What Will Be Deleted

When you submit a deletion request, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your account and authentication credentials
  • All characters you have created, including AI-generated portrait images
  • Your campaign history, story summaries, and game progress
  • Subscription status and purchase records stored on our servers
  • Any gameplay data associated with your account in Firestore
  • Abuse tracking records, if any exist for your account
⚠️ This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your data is deleted, your campaigns, characters, and progress cannot be recovered. If you have an active subscription, please cancel it in the App Store or Google Play before submitting this request — deletion of your account does not automatically cancel your subscription.

What Is Not Deleted

The following data may be retained after deletion:

  • Transaction records — payment processor records held by Apple, Google, or RevenueCat for legal and accounting purposes
  • Anonymized analytics — aggregate data that cannot be linked back to you individually
  • Legal holds — if required by applicable law
